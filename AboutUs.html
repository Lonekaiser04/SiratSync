Our Mission

SiratSync is built with a single, heartfelt mission: to help Muslims strengthen their connection with Allah through daily technology that inspires, reminds, and facilitates spiritual growth.

In today's fast-paced digital world, we believe technology should serve our deen, not distract from it. SiratSync is designed to be a digital companion that brings you closer to the Qur'an, makes your worship more meaningful, and helps you build consistent Islamic habits that bring peace and barakah to your life.

We aim to create a holistic Islamic lifestyle experience that supports you from Fajr to Isha, transforming your smartphone from a potential distraction into a tool for spiritual elevation and connection with your faith.

Why SiratSync Was Created

The journey of SiratSync began with a simple observation: while there are countless apps for productivity, entertainment, and social connection, there were very few comprehensive, beautifully designed Islamic apps that truly understood the needs of modern Muslims.

Most existing solutions felt outdated, fragmented, or lacked the thoughtful design that makes daily use a pleasure rather than a chore. We envisioned something different—an app that would:

  • Make the Qur'an accessible and beautiful to read daily
  • Ensure Muslims never miss a prayer with gentle, reliable reminders
  • Help build and track meaningful Islamic habits
  • Provide authentic Islamic content in a modern, user-friendly format
  • Create a sense of spiritual community without compromising privacy

SiratSync was born from this vision—to create a spiritual toolkit that feels both ancient in its wisdom and contemporary in its execution, serving as a bridge between timeless faith and modern life.

What Makes SiratSync Unique

While there are many Islamic apps available, SiratSync stands apart through its thoughtful integration of features, commitment to authenticity, and focus on user experience:

Holistic Approach: Unlike apps that focus on just one aspect of Islamic practice, SiratSync brings together Qur'an, prayer, dhikr, hadith, duas, and habit tracking in one seamless experience.

Thoughtful Design: Every interface, animation, and interaction is designed with spiritual intentionality—calming colors, elegant typography, and intuitive navigation that enhances rather than distracts from your worship.

Privacy-First: We believe your spiritual journey is personal. SiratSync collects minimal data, doesn't show ads, and never sells user information. Your trust is our most valued asset.

Authenticity & Accuracy: All Islamic content is sourced from reputable, verified references. We work with Islamic scholars to ensure translations, prayer times, and religious content meet high standards of authenticity.

Core Features

SiratSync brings together everything you need for daily spiritual practice in one beautifully integrated experience:

Qur'an Mushaf

Complete Qur'an with Uthmani & Indo-Pak scripts, translations, bookmarks, and reading progress tracking.

Prayer Times

Accurate prayer times with multiple calculation methods, Qibla direction, and mosque finder.

Adhan Notifications

Reliable prayer reminders with beautiful Adhan options and customizable alert times.

Duas Collection

Comprehensive collection of authentic duas for daily life occasions with transliteration and translation.

Hadith Library

Curated collection of authentic hadith with search, favorites, and daily hadith feature.

Digital Tasbih

Beautiful digital tasbih counter with multiple dhikr options and progress tracking.

Habit Tracker

Build and track Islamic habits with motivational streaks, goals, and progress visualization.

Community

Share encouragement, join group challenges, and connect with other Muslims on their spiritual journeys.

Offline Mode

Access Qur'an, prayers, and essential features without an internet connection.

Theme Customization

Choose from light, dark, or sepia themes with adjustable fonts for comfortable reading.

Multilingual Support

Available in English, Urdu, Hindi,,Malay and Arabic with more languages coming soon.

Our Commitment to Authenticity & Privacy

We understand that when it comes to matters of faith, trust is paramount. That's why we've built SiratSync on two foundational pillars:

Authenticity First

All Islamic content in SiratSync is sourced from reputable, verified references:

  • Qur'an: Using the widely recognized Madinah Mushaf with verification from multiple authoritative sources
  • Hadith: Sourced primarily from Sahih Bukhari and Sahih Muslim with clear grading indicators
  • Duas: Collected from authentic books of supplication with proper references
  • Prayer Times: Calculated using established methods with options for different madhahib
Verified Sources Accurate Translation Continuous Verification

Your Privacy is Sacred

We believe your spiritual journey is personal and private. SiratSync is designed with a privacy-first approach:

  • Minimal Data Collection: We only collect what's absolutely necessary for app functionality
  • No Selling of Data: We will never sell or share your personal information with third parties
  • Local Storage: Most of your data (bookmarks, prayer logs, etc.) stays on your device
  • Transparent Permissions: We clearly explain why each permission is needed and allow you to opt out
  • Ad-Free Experience: SiratSync is completely free of advertisements and tracking

"Your trust is our amanah (trust from Allah). We strive to honor that trust in every line of code and every design decision."
