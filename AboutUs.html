What is SiratSync?

A comprehensive Islamic app designed to help Muslims stay connected with their faith through technology

Complete Quran

Read Quran with beautiful Arabic script, English translation, and adjustable settings. Bookmark ayahs and track your reading progress.

Accurate Prayer Times

Never miss a prayer with auto-calculated prayer times based on your location, Qibla direction, and customizable reminders.

Authentic Hadith

Access authentic Hadith collections from Sahih Bukhari and Sahih Muslim with easy browsing and search functionality.

Duas Collection

Comprehensive collection of authentic duas from Quran and Sunnah for all occasions, with Arabic text and translations.

Habit Tracker

Build and track your Islamic habits like Salah, Quran reading, fasting, and dhikr with streak tracking and progress monitoring.

Our Mission

To create technology that serves the Ummah by making Islamic practices easier, more accessible, and integrated into daily life. We believe in using modern technology to strengthen our connection with Allah.

Meet the Developer

The mind and mission behind SiratSync

Kaiser Mohiuddin

Computer Science Student • Full-Stack & Android Developer

SiratSync was built as a passion-driven initiative to bridge modern technology with Islamic values. As a Muslim developer, I identified the need for a reliable and thoughtfully designed Islamic app that supports consistency in faith while fitting naturally into today’s digital lifestyle.

With a background in computer science and a strong interest in building practical, user-focused applications, I aimed to create a platform that combines authenticity, simplicity, and usability. Many existing Islamic apps either lack consistency, modern design, or long-term maintenance. SiratSync was developed to address these gaps by offering essential Islamic features in a single, clean, and dependable application.

The project began as a personal learning journey and gradually evolved through continuous improvement, real-world usage, and user feedback. Every feature in SiratSync is designed with intention—focused on helping users build sustainable habits, stay connected to their faith, and benefit from technology without distraction.

My long-term goal is to continue improving SiratSync as a service to the Ummah by maintaining quality, transparency, and ethical development practices, while learning and growing as a developer through meaningful, purpose-driven projects.
