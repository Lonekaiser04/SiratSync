Instructions for deleting your account and understanding how Google login works

How to Delete Your Account

To delete your SiratSync account, follow these steps:

  1. Open the SiratSync app.
  2. Go to Settings from the top of the screen.
  3. Scroll down to the bottom section labeled Danger Zone.
  4. Tap on Delete Account.
  5. A confirmation screen will appear showing what data will be permanently removed.

This process cannot be undone once completed.

When Using Google Login

If you signed in using your Google account, you do NOT need a password to delete your account. Google accounts rely on your most recent login session.

If your session is old or expired, your device may ask you to verify your Google account again before continuing. No password is required from SiratSync.

When Using Email/Password Login

If you created your account using an email and password, you will be asked to enter your password to confirm deletion.

Data That Will Be Deleted

Deleting your account will permanently remove:

  • Your profile information
  • All posts, comments, likes, and activities
  • Followers and following data
  • All habits and streak information
  • Account settings and app preferences
  • Login activity and user ID
  • Access to premium features
