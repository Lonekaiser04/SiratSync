Delete Your SiratSync Account

Step-by-step guide with visual instructions for account deletion

Important: Irreversible Action

Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, including habits, streaks, and progress, will be permanently removed.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Open the SiratSync app on your device
  2. Navigate to Settings from the main menu
  3. Scroll to the Danger Zone section
  4. Tap on Delete Account
  5. Confirm your decision on the verification screen

Google Login Users

No password required from SiratSync when deleting your account.

If your Google session has expired, you may be prompted to re-authenticate with Google before proceeding.

Email/Password Users

You will be asked to enter your account password to confirm deletion.

This is a security measure to prevent unauthorized account deletion.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your SiratSync account, the following data will be permanently removed from our servers:

  • Your complete user profile and account information
  • All habit tracking data and streak progress
  • App settings and customization preferences
  • Community posts and interactions (if applicable)
  • All analytics and usage data associated with your account

Consider Alternatives

Before deleting your account, consider these alternatives:

  • Take a break: Simply uninstall the app and reinstall later
  • Reset data: Clear your progress while keeping your account
  • Disable notifications: Turn off reminders instead of deleting
