Quran Features

What Quran translations are available in SiratSync?

SiratSync currently includes the Quran with English translation. We are working on adding more translations based on user demand.

You can view the translation alongside the Arabic text for better understanding and learning.

Can I read Quran offline?

Yes! The complete Quran text is available offline. Once downloaded, you can read it anytime without an internet connection.

This includes both Arabic text and English translation, making it perfect for travel or areas with limited connectivity.

Can I bookmark verses and save my reading progress?

Absolutely! SiratSync allows you to:

  • Bookmark your favorite verses for quick access
  • Save your reading progress
  • Add surahs to favorites for quick access

All bookmarks and progress are saved locally on your device for privacy.

Prayer Times & Qibla Direction

How accurate are the prayer times?

SiratSync calculates prayer times based on your exact location using established calculation methods. You can choose from:

  • Umm al-Qura (Makkah)
  • Muslim World League
  • Egyptian General Authority
  • ISNA (North America)
  • Shia Ithna-Ashari

Times are updated automatically based on your location changes.

How do prayer notifications work?

SiratSync provides reliable prayer reminders using Android's notification system:

  • Notifications 10 minutes before each prayer (customizable)
  • Adhan alerts at prayer time
  • Background scheduling for reliability
  • Customizable notification sounds

You can adjust notification timing and sounds in the app settings.

How does the Qibla direction feature work?

The Qibla compass uses your device's sensors to show the direction to Kaaba:

  • High-accuracy compass with visual indicators
  • Works online and offline (once calibrated)
  • Shows distance to Makkah
  • Calibration guidance for best accuracy

Make sure to calibrate your compass by moving your phone in a figure-8 motion for best results.

Hadith Collections & Duas

Which Hadith collections are included?

SiratSync includes authentic Hadith from:

  • Sahih al-Bukhari
  • Sahih Muslim

All Hadith are presented with proper references and are available offline. More collections are planned for future updates.

How can I access Duas for different occasions?

You can go to Dua collection and you can see or search:

  • Morning & Evening Duas
  • Prayer Duas
  • Daily Life Duas
  • Special Occasions

Each dua includes Arabic text and English translation. You can bookmark your favorite duas for quick access.

What is the Tasbih Counter feature?

The digital Tasbih Counter helps you track your dhikr:

  • Smooth counter with visual feedback
  • Vibration option with each count
  • Multiple dhikr options (Subhanallah, Alhamdulillah, Allahu Akbar)
  • Reset functionality for new sessions

Note: Counts are not saved between sessions to maintain simplicity and privacy.

Islamic Habit Tracker

What habits can I track with SiratSync?

SiratSync helps you track various Islamic habits including:

  • Salah - Daily prayers
  • Quran Reading - Daily verses/pages
  • Fasting - Optional/Sunnah fasts
  • Dhikr - Daily remembrance
  • Charity - Sadaqah tracking

You can customize which habits to track based on your personal goals.

How does streak tracking work?

Streaks help motivate you to maintain consistency:

  • Visual streak counters for each habit
  • Achievement badges for milestones
  • Progress charts showing your consistency
  • Personalized encouragement

Streaks are designed to be motivating without being stressful - there's always room for starting fresh.

Is my habit data private?

No,but you can set it to private!:

  • Not shared with anyone
  • You can delete it anytime
  • Stored securely on your device and our servers

You have the option to make your streaks public or private..

Technical Support & Settings

What languages does SiratSync support?

SiratSync is available in multiple languages:

  • English
  • Urdu
  • Hindi
  • Arabic
  • Malay

You can change the app language in Settings. More languages are planned based on user requests.

How can I customize the app appearance?

SiratSync offers various customization options:

  • Themes: Light, Dark, and Emerald themes
  • Font Size: Adjust Quran and text sizes
  • Display Settings: Customize prayer time display
  • Notification Settings: Customize alerts and sounds

All settings are easily accessible from the Settings menu.

What should I do if the app crashes or has issues?

If you experience any issues:

  1. Restart the app - Close and reopen SiratSync
  2. Update the app - Check for updates in Play Store
  3. Clear cache - Go to phone Settings → Apps → SiratSync → Clear Cache
  4. Reinstall - If issues persist, uninstall and reinstall from Play Store
  5. Contact Support - Email siratsync@gmail.com with details

Most issues can be resolved with a simple restart or update.

How do I delete my account and data?

To delete your account and all associated data:

  1. Go to Settings → Account
  2. Select "Delete Account"
  3. Confirm your choice
  4. All your data will be permanently deleted

Alternatively, you can email us at siratsync@gmail.com with your request. Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Note: Local app data on your device may need to be cleared separately through your phone's app settings.
