SiratSync FAQ

Your Spiritual Companion for Qur'an, Prayer, and Islamic Reminders

Welcome to SiratSync, your comprehensive Islamic mobile companion. This FAQ will help you understand all the features, settings, and capabilities of our app. If you have additional questions, please reach out through our in-app support.

Quran Reading & Features

Does SiratSync offer Quran translations?

Yes! SiratSync includes translations in English. You can view the translation alongside the Arabic text or as a separate pane.

Can I read Quran offline?

Absolutely! SiratSync includes the complete Quran text, translations.

Prayer Times & Reminders

How accurate are the prayer times in SiratSync?

SiratSync calculates prayer times based on your precise location using multiple calculation methods (Umm al-Qura, ISNA, Muslim World League, etc.). You can select your preferred calculation method in settings.

By default, SiratSync provides notifications 10 minutes before each prayer time to help you prepare but you can change this in settings to recieve notification before 30,20 minutes etc.

How do Adhan alerts and background notifications work?

SiratSync uses Android's WorkManager for reliable, battery-efficient scheduling of prayer notifications. Even if the app is closed, you'll receive Adhan alerts at the correct times.

You can customize the Adhan sound, vibration pattern, and notification style for prayer times. The app also provides "Time to Pray" reminders before each prayer time.

Does SiratSync track my prayer history?

Yes, the Islamic Habit Tracker includes prayer logging. You can mark prayers as completed, make up missed prayers, and view your prayer consistency over time with beautiful visual charts.

This feature is designed to encourage consistency without judgment, helping you build and maintain your prayer habit.

Daily Inspiration & Tracking

What are "Verse of the Day" and "Hadith of the Day"?

Each day, SiratSync presents you with an inspiring Verse of the Day and Hadith of the Day to start your morning with spiritual reflection.

How does the Islamic Habit Tracker work?

The Habit Tracker helps you build consistent Islamic practices like prayer, Quran reading, fasting, and charity. You set goals, log your activities, and track progress with motivational streaks and achievements.

It's designed with Islamic psychology in mind, focusing on gradual improvement and Allah's mercy rather than perfectionism.

Does the Tasbih Counter save my counts?

No! The Tasbih Counter does not save your counts between sessions to maintain your privacy and simplicity.

The counter includes beautiful visual feedback and optional vibration with each count to enhance your mindfulness during dhikr.

Privacy, Sync & Technical

Is my data private and secure with SiratSync?

Absolutely. SiratSync is designed with privacy as a core Islamic value. Your prayer logs, Quran bookmarks, and personal data never leave your device.

We don't collect personal information, track your app usage for advertising, or share data with third parties. The app is completely ad-free.

What permissions does SiratSync need and why?

SiratSync requests minimal permissions:

  • Location: For accurate prayer times and Qibla direction
  • Notifications: For prayer alerts and spiritual reminders
  • Storage: To download Aurad e fatiha for offline use

You can deny any permission, though some features may be limited. We never access contacts, messages, or other personal data.

Can I customize the app's appearance?

Yes! SiratSync offers multiple themes including light, dark, and sepia modes. You can adjust font sizes, choose Quran page styles, and select accent colors to create a comfortable reading experience.

The app respects system dark mode settings and can automatically adjust based on time of day or your preference.

Community & Future Features

What is the Community feature?

The Community section allows you to share inspiring verses,hadiths , reflections and other islamic content.

All community interactions are moderated to ensure respectful, beneficial discussions in line with Islamic principles. This feature is completely optional.

Is SiratSync available in any other Languages ?

Yes, SiratSync supports multiple languages including Arabic, English, Urdu,Hindi and Malay. We are continuously adding new translations to make the app accessible to a wider audience.

You can change the app language in settings, and Quran translations are available in only English.

What features are planned for future updates?

Based on user feedback, we're working on:

  • Advanced Quran study tools with tafsir integration
  • Ramadan-specific features including I'tikaf tracker
  • Enhanced audio features with famous Qaris
  • Zakat calculator and charity tracking

We welcome your suggestions through the in-app feedback form!
